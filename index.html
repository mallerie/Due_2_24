Top Ten Best/Worst Disney Role Models

Not all Disney characters are created equally.

Jump to the Worst Role Models

The Best

  1. Mulan

    As if these reasons aren't enough, Mulan makes the top of the list because she does it all. She is dedicated to her family and friends, who can rely on her when they need to. Don't forget, though, that this girl saved China. It doesn't get more bad ass than a woman saving the most populous country on earth.

  2. Tiana

    We could write a book about what makes Tiana a great role model, but we know you have many other role models to learn about so we will leave you with this. Some say Tiana is one of the most underated Disney princesses, and we agree. She is hard-working, independent, devoted to family and supports her community all at the same time. There aint nothin like a self-made woman.

  3. Pocahantas

    How can anyone deny the awesomeness that is Pocahantas? She is fierce, strong, wise and brave. Though disney made a few mistakes with the historical acurateness of this film, Pocahantas still makes it to the top three. Learn more here.

  4. Sheriff Woody

    Woody made the list because not only is he hard-working and a dedicated leader of the toys, he even made Fortune magazine's World's Best Leaders list.

  5. James P. Sullivan

    Sulley is a great friend, fun to be around and is great with children. He makes the list because of how he shines at work.

  6. Mr. Incredible

    Great dad, hero and leader. He had to overcome his ego in order to work with the other Supers to save the day. And he saved his family and the city in the process.

  7. Dory

    Though her memory is all but useless, Dory makes the best sidekick anyone could ask for. She is loyal, sometimes dependable, and cares about family.

  8. Jessie

    Jessie is definitely a fan favorite, and for a good reason, too. She is kind, loyal and funny and playful. There are eventheories about where her character comes from that will blow your mind.

  9. Carl Fredricksen

    Carl warmed our hearts when he found himself again after the death of his wife. He is a role model to us all that life truly can go on.

  10. Bruce (From Finding Nemo)

    Bruce made the list despite his short comings. He makes a good role model for his work in encouraging other sharks to see their fish neighbors as friends, not as food. He's so cool that real sharks even want to look like him.

The Worst

  1. Syndrome

    Syndrome is just a bad guy. Even when he was given a chance to be good, he didn't take it.

  2. Evil Emperor Zurg

    As long as his batteries are charged, this guy is out to do evil. He thrives off of hurting other toys and that aint cool.

  3. Lots-O'-Huggin' Bear (From Toy Story 3)

    Aside from being super scary, Huggin' Bear is a bad role model because he uses his innocent looks to fool unsuspecting toys into trusting him.

  4. Hopper

    Hopper is just plain bad. He has no problem killing ants, which is sad.

  5. Stinky Pete (From Toy Story 2)

    We all thought Stinky Pete would turn out to be a good father-figure for Andy, but he had other plans in mind.

  6. Scar (The Lion King)

    There is nothing worse than being betrayed by family. Nothing. Scar is a bad role model because he killed his brother, Mufasa.

  7. Ursula

    Ursula made the list because she actually enjoys being bad. Remember when she took Ariel's voice? That was fun for her. Bad things shouldn't be fun.

  8. Shan Yu

    Shan Yu invaded China and tried to take over the empire, killing hundreds of innocent people in the process. Shame on you, Shan Yu.

  9. Hans

    Hans gained Anna's trust, only to betray her in the end. Not cool, dude.

  10. Maleficent

    Maleficent poisoned Sleeping Beauty and tried to kill her all because she wasn't invited to the christening!
